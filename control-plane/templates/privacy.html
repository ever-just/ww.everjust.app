Privacy policy

The short version: your workspace data is yours, lives in its own database, and we don't track you. Here's the longer version.

What we collect

  • Account details — your email, organization name, and workspace address, collected at signup to create and administer your workspace.
  • Billing — handled entirely by Stripe. Card numbers never touch our servers; we receive only subscription status and receipts metadata.
  • Workspace content — whatever your team puts in your workspace. It's stored in your own isolated database and processed only to provide the service.
  • Operational logs — standard server logs (IP, timestamp, request path) kept briefly for security and reliability.

What we never do

  • No analytics or advertising trackers on this site or in your workspace.
  • No selling, sharing, pooling, or mining of your data.
  • No training of AI models on your content.
  • No third-party cookies.

Where your data lives

Each workspace runs on its own dedicated PostgreSQL database — no shared tenancy, no cross-workspace queries. Traffic is encrypted in transit (TLS), passwords are stored as salted hashes, and databases are backed up automatically. More in Security & data.

Your rights

  • Access & export — export records from any list view, bulk-download files, or request a full export.
  • Correction & deletion — edit or delete your data in the workspace anytime; on cancellation we delete workspace data on request or per retention schedule.
  • Questions or requests — email company@everjust.co and a human will handle it.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we'll note it here and, for significant changes, email workspace administrators.
