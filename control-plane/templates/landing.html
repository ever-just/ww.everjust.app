Questions, answered

How fast can we start?

Immediately. After checkout your workspace is provisioned automatically — usually in under 2 minutes — and you sign in with the email and password you chose at signup. No confirmation email gauntlet.

Is our data separated from other companies?

Yes. Every workspace runs on its own dedicated database. There is no shared tenancy at the data layer, and your workspace is only reachable at your own subdomain.

Does it work on phones?

Yes. The whole platform is responsive, and you can install it to your home screen as an app: on iPhone use Share → “Add to Home Screen”, on Android tap “Install app” in the browser menu.

What happens when we add people?

Your first 5 users are covered by the base $100/month. Each additional user is $15/month, prorated automatically — invite people whenever you need to.

Can we cancel?

Anytime, from your billing portal. Your subscription stops at the end of the billing period and you can export your data beforehand.

I already have a workspace — where do I sign in?

Go to everjust.app/signin and enter your workspace name, or visit your‑org.{{ domain }} directly and bookmark it.
