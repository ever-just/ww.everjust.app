The apps

One suite, one login, one shared set of contacts and files. Here's what each app is for.

{{ icon("chart-column", 18) }} CRM & Sales

Track leads through a pipeline you define, schedule follow-up activities, and convert won deals into quotes and orders. Every email, call, and note lands on the customer's record automatically, so anyone on the team can pick up the thread.

{{ icon("kanban", 18) }} Projects & Timesheets

Organize work into projects and tasks on kanban boards, assign owners and deadlines, and discuss in the task itself instead of a separate chat. Log hours directly on tasks; timesheets roll up per project and per person — and feed payroll where relevant.

{{ icon("folder-open", 18) }} Documents

A full document management system: storages, nested folders, tags, and categories, with access groups controlling who can see, edit, or delete each directory. Files attached anywhere in the workspace are searchable here, and multiple attachments can be downloaded as a single ZIP.

{{ icon("signature", 18) }} eSign

Upload a PDF, place signature and text fields by role, and send it out for legally-binding electronic signature. Track every envelope to completion, and find the signed copies back in Documents.

{{ icon("book-open", 18) }} Knowledge

Wiki-style pages for your internal documentation — onboarding guides, processes, policies. Pages support categories, templates, full version history, and optional approval workflows, and can be linked to contacts.

{{ icon("users", 18) }} HR, Time Off & Attendance

Employee records with contracts, leave types and approval flows for time off, and clock-in/clock-out attendance. Managers approve requests from their inbox; calendars show who's out when.

{{ icon("banknote", 18) }} Payroll

Salary structures and rules compute payslips from contracts — and worked days come straight from actual attendance records. Generate payslips in batches for the whole company and export the results for your accountant.

{{ icon("phone", 18) }} Calls & SMS

A softphone lives inside the workspace: click any phone number to dial, take notes during the call, and the call is logged on the contact. Two-way SMS works from the same records — send from any phone field, and replies thread into the conversation history.

How they fit together

  • Contacts are shared. The customer in CRM is the same record you invoice, sign documents with, call, and text.
  • Files are shared. Anything attached to a deal, task, or employee shows up in Documents with the same permissions.
  • Activity is shared. Every record has a chatter feed — emails, calls, SMS, and notes in one timeline.
