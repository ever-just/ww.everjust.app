Use it on your phone

The whole platform is responsive, and both the site and your workspace install to your home screen as an app — no app store needed.

iPhone & iPad (Safari)

  1. Open your‑org.{{ domain }} in Safari and sign in.
  2. Tap the Share button (the square with the arrow).
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen, then Add.

The EVERJUST icon appears on your home screen and opens full-screen, without browser chrome.

Android (Chrome)

  1. Open your‑org.{{ domain }} in Chrome and sign in.
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu, then Install app (or Add to Home screen).
  3. Confirm. Chrome may also offer an install banner automatically.

Tips for mobile work

  • Stay signed in. The installed app keeps your session, so it opens straight to your work.
  • Approve on the go. Time-off requests, document signatures, and task updates all work well on a phone.
  • Click-to-dial. Phone numbers in contacts are tappable — calls go through your phone with the record kept in the workspace.

Troubleshooting

  • No "Install app" option? Make sure you're in Safari (iOS) or Chrome (Android) — some in-app browsers (e.g. from a messaging app) can't install.
  • Old icon or stale page? Remove the home-screen app and add it again; it picks up the latest version.
